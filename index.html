Monkey Cage

A 5ft x 5ft area and 6ft high Monkey cage, built for a client who was keeping some cute small monkeys as pets. He wanted us to build it this large, so that he himself can enter inside and play with his pets. The cage is having double door access, and entirely movable on wheels.

Storage Rack

A 20ft x 12ft area and 8ft high storage rack, built for an Art store client. The rack is having 2 storage platforms, one at 4ft height and another at 8ft height.

Murphy Wall Bed

A 6ft x 4.5ft area Murphy wall bed, built for a client who wanted to save some space while even having a bed. So we offered him this murphy bed, gas operated. This can be folded back to wall, when not in use.

Marble Base Table

A 4ft high table with marble base and 12mm diameter supporting rods in hexagon shape.
